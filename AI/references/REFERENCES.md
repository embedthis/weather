## Weather APIs

- [Open-Meteo](https://open-meteo.com/) - Free weather API
  - [Geocoding API](https://open-meteo.com/en/docs/geocoding-api) - City to coordinates
  - [Weather API](https://open-meteo.com/en/docs) - Current weather and forecasts
  - [Weather Codes](https://open-meteo.com/en/docs#weathervariables) - WMO weather interpretation codes
